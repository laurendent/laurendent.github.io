Press Fit Wooden Stool

by Lauren Dent

This stool was designed with Fusion 360 and fabricated using a laser cutter to cut 1/4" wooden slabs. The pieces are "press fit" and ideally hold together without any glue.



Tales of Abstraction

by Lauren Dent, Mira Kaiser, Ava Tartakovsky, and Xinyue Zhou

Tales of Abstraction Installation & Animations

Tales of Abstraction is a collection of three animations, each telling the simple and abstract versions of well known fairytales Little Red Riding Hood, Cinderella, and Snow White. The animations were created in Blender, and displayed among other projects in a multimedia art installation in the UCSB Translab.
